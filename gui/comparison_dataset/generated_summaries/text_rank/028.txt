In the past , sports fans would be left debating the validity of the try for days and weeks to come . Viewers will be able to see precisely how the ball was thrown and by whom , giving a greater depth to the growing wealth of analysis available during sports broadcasts . BBC Sport is the first client to start using the system . The more cameras using Piero , the better the detail possible out the other end when the data is turned into 3D models . Piero also provides a wealth of statistical detail - from the length of kicks , to the length of a run of an individual player and the height of a lift at any lineout .
