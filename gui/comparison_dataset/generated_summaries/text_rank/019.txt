Slater to star in Broadway play Actor Christian Slater is stepping into the role of Tom in the Broadway revival of The Glass Menagerie . Slater , 35 , is replacing actor Dallas Roberts in the Tennessee Williams drama , which opens next month . The role will be played by understudy Joey Collins until Slater joins the show . Philip Rinaldi , a spokesman for the show , said the play 's 15 March opening date remains unchanged . The revival , directed by David Leveaux , will also star Jessica Lange as the domineering mother , Amanda Wingfield .
