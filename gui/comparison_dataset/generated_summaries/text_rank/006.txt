Japan narrowly escapes recession Japan 's economy teetered on the brink of a technical recession in the three months to September , figures show . Revised figures indicated growth of just 0.1 % - and a similar-sized contraction in the previous quarter . On an annual basis , the data suggests annual growth of just 0.2 % , suggesting a much more hesitant recovery than had previously been thought . The government was keen to play down the worrying implications of the data . But in the face of the strengthening yen making exports less competitive and indications of weakening economic conditions ahead , observers were less sanguine .
